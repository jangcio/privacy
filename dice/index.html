Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-10

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dice Mosaic mobile game. Dice Mosaic is a single-player puzzle game that can be used without account registration or login. The app may store gameplay records and settings on the device, and the release version may include third-party advertising.

1. Information We Collect

Dice Mosaic does not ask users to create an account or directly provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or payment details. However, in versions that display ads, advertising providers may process information such as advertising ID, device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, app interaction data, or diagnostic data for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

To support gameplay convenience, the app may store gameplay records or settings on the device, such as selected favorite color, high score, and other game preferences. This information is not linked to an account and is not sent by the core game to an external server for normal gameplay.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Dice Mosaic may display third-party advertising in the release version. When ads are served, the relevant ad network or service provider may process data for purposes such as ad delivery, frequency capping, measurement, and fraud prevention. Such data processing is governed by the privacy policy of the applicable third-party provider. Dice Mosaic does not directly sell users' personal information.

4. Retention and Deletion

Game settings and high scores stored on the device may be deleted when the user clears app data or removes the app. Retention periods and deletion procedures for information processed by advertising providers are subject to the policies of those providers.

5. Children's Privacy

Dice Mosaic does not knowingly collect children's personal information directly through account or profile features. If the release version includes advertising, data handling by advertising providers is subject to the policies and compliance obligations of those providers.
