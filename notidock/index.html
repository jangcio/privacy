Privacy Policy for NotiDock

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CoreWorks handles information in the NotiDock mobile app. NotiDock helps you review, search, snooze, archive, and organize notifications on your device.

1. Information NotiDock Processes

When you grant notification access, NotiDock may process notification information that appears on your device, including:

  • notification title and body text,
  • the app name and package name that generated the notification,
  • the notification received time, status, category, and snooze time,
  • rule matches and local organization metadata.

NotiDock also stores rules and preferences that you create in the app, such as selected apps, keywords, excluded keywords, categories, snooze settings, and retention settings.

2. Local Storage Only

NotiDock stores notification history, rules, and preferences locally on your device. This release does not create an account, require login, sync data to a CoreWorks server, or send your notification content to CoreWorks.

The Android release may include the standard Internet permission used by the app runtime, but the current release does not use it to transmit notification content or personal data to CoreWorks servers.

3. Third Parties, Ads, and Analytics

The current release of NotiDock does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or social login providers. We do not sell your data.

4. Permissions

NotiDock may ask you to enable notification access so it can read and organize notifications on your device. You can revoke notification access at any time in your Android system settings.

NotiDock may also request boot and battery optimization related access to keep local snooze and notification organization behavior working reliably on your device.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Notification history, rules, and preferences remain on your device until you delete them in the app, clear the app data, or uninstall the app. Because this release does not sync data to CoreWorks servers, CoreWorks cannot recover local data after it is deleted from your device.

6. Children

NotiDock is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We design NotiDock to keep notification content on your device. Please keep your device, operating system, and app version up to date to help protect local data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when NotiDock changes, when legal requirements change, or when new data-related features are added. When material changes are made, the "Last updated" date on this page will be updated.
